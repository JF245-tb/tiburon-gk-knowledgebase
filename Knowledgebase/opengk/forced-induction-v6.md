# 2.7L V6 Forced Induction Options
**Source:** https://opengk.org/index.php?title=2.7L_V6_Forced_Induction

---

## Turbocharger
Custom turbo builds recommended — all factory kits reached end-of-production in early 2010s. Community build documentation exists.

## Supercharger: Magnuson MP62

Progressive stages that build on each other:

| Stage | WHP | PSI | Key Features | Tuning Required |
|-------|-----|-----|-------------|-----------------|
| Stage 0 | 210 | 2-3 | Eaton MP62, 3.4" pulley, intake manifold | No |
| Stage I | 225 | 2-3 | Adds intercooler, heat exchanger, pump | No |
| Stage II | 260-270 | 5-6 | Upgraded injectors, 2.8" pulley | Yes (reflash) |
| Stage III | 280-300 | 8-10 | 6.5" crank pulley | Yes (stresses stock parts) |
| Stage IV | 320-340 | 12-13 | 7" crank pulley | Standalone ECM required |

## Supercharger: Magnuson MP90
- Higher efficiency than MP62
- 370-385 WHP at 7 PSI standard
- 400+ WHP potential
- Includes 460cc injectors and dedicated fuel tuning system
