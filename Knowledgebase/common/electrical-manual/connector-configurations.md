## Key Engine Harness Connectors (CC-6+)

For V6 engine management, the most-used connectors in the CC section:

| Connector | Component | Notes |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| C133-1 | ECM (Siemens SIMK43) — main 26-pin | Fuel, ignition, sensors |
| C133-2 | ECM — secondary connector | Additional I/O |
| C109 | Transaxle / VSS | 4-pulse Hall IC speed sensor |
