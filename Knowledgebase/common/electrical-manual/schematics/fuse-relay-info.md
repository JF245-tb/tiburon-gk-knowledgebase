## Notes

- SD-2 and SD-4 show front-view physical layout diagrams of the BCM fuse box with relay and fuse positions.
- SD-6 shows the rear-view layout of the BCM box showing relay locations (door lock, unlock, defogger, headlamp, A/C relay, etc.).
- The E/R (Engine/Relay) box is in the engine compartment; the BCM box is in the passenger compartment (driver side kick panel area).
- BCM box fuses numbered 1-27 serve interior and body circuits.
- E/R box fusible links protect high-current feeds from the battery/generator.
- E/R box fuses protect engine management and front lighting circuits.
