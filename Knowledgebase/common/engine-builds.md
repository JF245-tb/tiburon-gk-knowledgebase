## Build 1: "Milled Heads" Engine (Budget/Experimental)

### Purpose
Experimental build to see what happens with increased compression from milled heads. Lower-priority engine.

### Block
- Second-best block from parts inventory
- Stock bore (not overbored)

### Heads
- Sourced from third parts car
- Internals were in near-new condition wear-wise
- **Corrosion** on heads from being stored outside for years
- Machine shop milled **3 thousandths** off the deck surface
- Machine shop was going to scrap them — kept for experimental use
- Expected result: slightly increased compression ratio from reduced combustion chamber volume

### Rotating Assembly
- Stock (not balanced)
- Stock pistons
- Stock connecting rods

### Notes
- This is the "let's see what happens" build
- The 3 thou mill is modest — primary concern was cleaning up the corroded surface
- Will produce slightly higher compression than stock; monitor for detonation when tuning
