BR »30                                                        BRAKE SYSTEM
The Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS) controls the hydraulic
brake pressure of all four wheels during braking on haz-
ardous road surfaces, preventing the wheels from locking
up. ABS provides the following benefits.

1.   Enables steering around obstacles with a greater de-
     gree of certainty, even during emergency braking.
2.   Enables stopping during emergency braking while
     keeping stability and steerability even on curves.

If a malfunction occurs, a diagnosis function and fail-safe
system are included for serviceability.

The Hydraulic and Electronic Control Unit (HECU) re-
ceives signals about the vehicle's speed, direction and
road conditions from sensors at the wheels.

Based on these signals, the control unit will determine the
optimum amount of wheel spin.
