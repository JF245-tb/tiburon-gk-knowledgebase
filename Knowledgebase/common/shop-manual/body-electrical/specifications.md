##### Fuel Gauge

| Items | Specifications |
|---|---|
| Type | Cross-coil type (Float point type). Pointer should not fall into the "F" point but indicate remaining fuel level when the ignition is off. |

**Standard Values:**

| Level | Gauge | Resistance (&Omega;) | Gauge Angle (°) |
|---|---|---|---|
| E (Empty) | | 97 | -45 +/- 2.5 |
| F | | 10.5 | 0 |
| F (Full) | | 6 | 45 +/- 2.5 |

> Inspection order: OFF &rarr; E &rarr; F &rarr; E

> The level must be reached within 7 minutes after the resistance is set for Full or Empty.

> Point stability tolerance: Within off.

> Apply power for 10 minutes. Then turn off the power for an hour and read the position of the pointer.
