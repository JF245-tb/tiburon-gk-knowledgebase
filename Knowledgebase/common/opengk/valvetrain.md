# 2.7L V6 Valvetrain Specifications
**Source:** https://opengk.org/index.php?title=2.7L_V6_Valvetrain

---

## Valve Springs

### OEM 2.7L Delta
- Inner diameter: 17.50mm
- Outer diameter: 24.70mm
- Installed height: 34.5mm
- Pressure @ installed height: 45 lbs
- Pressure @ 8mm lift: 100-105 lbs
- Pressure @ 8.9mm lift: 110-115 lbs

## Retainers & Keepers
- OEM retainers designed for 17.50mm I.D. spring
- 9-degree keeper angle
- 6mm valve stems

**⚠️ Compatibility Warning:** Aftermarket retainers may have mismatched keeper angles, valve stem diameters, or incorrect keeper grooves. Some aftermarket springs require cylinder head machining and may have oversized inner diameters incompatible with stock retainers. Verify compatibility before ordering.
