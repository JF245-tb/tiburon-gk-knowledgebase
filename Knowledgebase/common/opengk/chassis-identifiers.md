## Notes

- On ECU part number labels, only the **first letter** of the chassis identifier appears (e.g., "G" for GK Tiburon)
- Cross-flash compatibility is generally limited to the same chassis (GK only for Tiburon)
- See `ecm-identification.md` for how to read ECU labels and determine variant
